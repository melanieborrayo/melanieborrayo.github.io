Currently, the world of philosophy has taken over my career, and I have invested time into learning all the ways in which I can apply the teachings of ethics into every opportunity. Overall, my professional career has grown to cater to the application of knowledge and wisdom in every situation. I understand that it is important to follow the teachings of philosophy closely and all my experiences in education closely reflect that. I have worked to improve my ability to apply critical thinking and problem solving skills to all aspects of life and I intend to continue understanding life from an ethical point of view. So far, I believe I have developed the ability to produce ethical ideas and apply them to all sorts of encounters. My capacity to complete work and engage in philosophy has been secured through the experiences I have encountered and continue to seek.
